My Experiences

" My journey is a tapestry woven with diverse experiences and educational milestones. As an Assistant Land Surveyor, I actively participated in land measurement processes. My tenure as a Logistics Operations Specialist at MALAYSIA POST and a Sales Assistant at 7 Eleven further polished my adaptability and decision-making skills. Proficient in Microsoft Office, Photoshop, video editing, and ICT, I bring not just technical skills but a proven ability to work under pressure, adapt, and make informed decisions. Fluent in Malay, English, and Indonesian, I am ready to embrace new challenges and contribute my diverse skill set to meaningful projects. "
